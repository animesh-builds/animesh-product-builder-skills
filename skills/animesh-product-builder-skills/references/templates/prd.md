## Target users
Persona or two. Concrete, not generic.

## Prior art / competitive landscape
What already exists in this space and why this is different. One paragraph.

## User stories

- **P1 (soon after)**
- **P2 (nice to have)**

## Success metrics
How do we know this worked? Quantitative where possible.

## Risks
